About SatLoom

SatLoom is a free, open-source communication platform designed to connect people seamlessly through video calls, audio calls, and text chats.

Version: 1.0.0

Developed by: WebRTC Developers

Purpose: To provide a simple, secure, and accessible way to communicate in real-time using WebRTC technology.

For more information or to contribute, visit our GitHub repository.

Our Mission

SatLoom was created to provide free communication tools to everyone, regardless of location or device. Our goal is to make high-quality communication accessible to all.
